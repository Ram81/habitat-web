Instructions

  • In this task, you will be asked to perform simple activities -- e.g. “find and go to the cabinet” -- in a simulated household environment.

  • You will be able to move around in the environment using keyboard controls.

  • Before the task begins, you will be shown a task demo to familiarize you with the task setup. You will also be able to use and practice the keyboard controls at this time.

  • At the end of the task, you will be prompted to complete a survey.

  • Do not navigate away or reload the webpage until the HIT is complete.

  • The task will take approximately 2-4 minutes to load in your browser, consisting of a “Downloading” phase and then a “Running” phase. The start button will be enabled once downloading has completed

  • To summarize, you will go through four stages during this HIT:

    1. Task demo
    2. Training task
    3. Actual task
    4. Survey

  • You have to go through the task demo and training task only once. You will start with the actual task directly in subsequent HITs.
